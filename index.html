Venezuela

Venezuela, officially the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela, is a tropical country located in northern South America, with a coastline on the Caribbean Sea. Known for its immense biodiversity, it is home to Angel Falls (the world's highest waterfall), the Andes Mountains, vast plains, and the Amazon rainforest. Its economy is based on the world's largest oil reserves.
